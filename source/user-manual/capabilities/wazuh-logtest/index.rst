Wazuh-Logtest
=============

.. versionadded:: 4.1.0

The Wazuh-Logtest whole solution was designed to replace ossec-logtest,
now allowing to test and verify rules and decoders remotely, sharing the rules engine
with :ref:`wazuh-analysisd <wazuh-analysisd>`
